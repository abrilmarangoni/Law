About Zach

Zach Lewis is an Associate in Saretsky Hart Michaels + Gould PC’s Chicago office. Zach graduated from Indiana University Maurer School of Law with Order of the Barrister’s honors. While in law school, he served as the Chief Justice of all advocacy programs at Maurer and competed representing his school in the Anderson Center Seventh Circuit Moot Court Competition in Chicago. Prior to SHMG, Zach also worked at a large financial firm on litigation matters pending against the firm.
